## General Questions

### What is Community Manager?

Community Manager is a comprehensive condo/HOA association management platform with a unique WebOS-inspired interface. It provides all the tools needed to manage a residential community, including messaging, calendar, tasks, documents, and resident directory.

### Who is it for?

- **Condominium Associations** - Any size
- **Homeowners Associations (HOAs)** - Small to mid-size
- **Property Management Companies** - Managing multiple associations
- **Board Members** - Running association governance
- **Residents** - Staying connected with community

### What makes it different from other HOA software?

1. **Modern Interface** - WebOS-inspired design, not stuck in 2005
2. **True Responsive** - Actually works well on mobile
3. **All-in-One** - Everything in one platform
4. **Intuitive** - Minimal training needed
5. **Affordable** - Competitive pricing

### Is it really inspired by WebOS?

Yes! We studied Palm/HP WebOS design principles and implemented:
- Card-based multitasking
- Three-pane layouts
- Gesture navigation
- Progressive disclosure
- Unified design language

### What's the pricing?

Pricing is typically per-unit based:
- **Starter**: $1-2 per unit/month (up to 50 units)
- **Professional**: $0.75-1.50 per unit/month (51-200 units)
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing (200+ units)

Contact your administrator or Abacus.AI for exact pricing.

## Account & Access

### How do I get an account?

Accounts are created by your association administrator. Contact your board or property manager to request access.

### I forgot my password. What do I do?

1. Go to login page
2. Click "Forgot password?"
3. Enter your email
4. Check email for reset link
5. Follow instructions to reset

### Can I have multiple accounts?

Generally no - one account per user. However, if you manage multiple associations, your administrator can configure multi-organization access.

### What are the different user roles?

1. **Super Admin** - Platform-wide access (rarely used)
2. **Admin** - Full association management
3. **Board Member** - Governance and oversight access
4. **Resident** - Standard community member access

### How do I change my role?

You can't change your own role. Contact your administrator to request a role change.

### Can I access from multiple devices?

Yes! Access from:
- Desktop computers (Mac, Windows, Linux)
- Laptops
- Tablets (iPad, Android tablets)
- Phones (iPhone, Android)

Your data syncs across all devices automatically.

## Features & Functionality

### Messages

**Q: How do I send a message?**
A: Open Messages → New Message → Select recipient(s) → Type and send

**Q: Can I message multiple people?**
A: Yes! Select multiple recipients when composing.

**Q: How do I attach files?**
A: Click the paperclip icon when composing a message.

**Q: Can I delete messages?**
A: You can delete your own sent messages. Received messages can be archived.

**Q: Are messages private?**
A: Yes, only you and the recipients can see your messages. Admins cannot read private messages.

### Calendar

**Q: Can I create recurring events?**
A: Yes! When creating an event, enable "Recurring" and set frequency.

**Q: How do I share my calendar?**
A: Community calendars are automatically shared. Personal calendar stays private.

**Q: Can I export events?**
A: Yes! Events can be exported to .ics format.

**Q: Who can create events?**
A: Board members and admins can create community events. Everyone can create personal events.

**Q: How do I set reminders?**
A: When creating/editing an event, set notification time (e.g., 15 minutes before).

### Tasks

**Q: Can I assign tasks to others?**
A: Yes! When creating a task, select assignee from dropdown.

**Q: Can I set due dates?**
A: Yes! Click calendar icon when creating a task.

**Q: How do I organize tasks?**
A: Use Lists (left pane) to organize by category/project.

**Q: Can I add subtasks?**
A: Yes! Open a task and add checklist items.

**Q: What do priority colors mean?**
A: Red = High, Yellow = Medium, Blue = Low priority.

### Notes

**Q: Are my notes private?**
A: Yes, unless you explicitly share them.

**Q: Can I format text?**
A: Yes! Bold, italic, lists, and links are supported.

**Q: How do I find old notes?**
A: Use search bar or browse by category.

**Q: Do notes auto-save?**
A: Yes! Changes save automatically as you type.

**Q: Can I add images to notes?**
A: Currently text-only. Use Documents for file storage.

### Documents

**Q: What file types can I upload?**
A: PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, images, text files, and more.

**Q: Is there a file size limit?**
A: Typically 50MB per file. Contact admin if you need larger.

**Q: Where are files stored?**
A: Securely in AWS S3 cloud storage.

**Q: Can I share documents?**
A: Yes! Right-click document → Share → Select recipients.

**Q: How do I create folders?**
A: Click "+ New Folder" button and name your folder.

**Q: Can I download multiple files at once?**
A: Currently one at a time. Bulk download coming soon.

### Directory

**Q: How do I update my contact info?**
A: My Household → Profile Tab → Edit Information

**Q: Who can see my information?**
A: Other residents can see basic info (name, unit). Detailed info visible to admins only.

**Q: How do I join a committee?**
A: Contact your board or use "Request Update" feature.

**Q: Can I hide my phone number?**
A: Yes! Settings → Privacy → Make phone private.

**Q: How do I find someone's contact info?**
A: Directory app → Search for name or unit number.

## Mobile & Desktop

### What's the difference between mobile and desktop?

**Desktop:**
- Multiple windows open simultaneously
- Drag and drop windows
- Window snapping
- Larger screens = more visible content

**Mobile:**
- One app at a time (full screen)
- Card-based switching
- Gesture navigation
- Optimized touch targets

### Can I use gestures on desktop?**
A: Trackpad gestures work on Mac. Otherwise use keyboard shortcuts.

### How do I switch apps on mobile?

Swipe up from bottom → See all open apps → Tap to switch

### Can I install it as an app?

Yes! It's a Progressive Web App (PWA):

**iOS:**
1. Open in Safari
2. Tap Share button
3. "Add to Home Screen"

**Android:**
1. Open in Chrome
2. Menu → "Install app"
or
2. Banner appears → Tap "Install"

**Desktop:**
- Chrome/Edge: Address bar → Install icon

### Does it work offline?

Partially. Installed PWA can:
- View previously loaded content
- Queue actions to sync later
- Show cached documents

Full functionality requires internet connection.

### What browsers are supported?

**Fully Supported:**
- Chrome (latest)
- Edge (latest)
- Safari (latest)
- Firefox (latest)

**Mobile:**
- iOS Safari (iOS 14+)
- Chrome Android (latest)

## Troubleshooting

### App won't load / white screen

1. **Refresh the page** (Cmd+R / Ctrl+R)
2. **Clear cache**: Browser settings → Clear browsing data
3. **Try incognito/private mode**
4. **Different browser**: Try Chrome if using other browser
5. **Check internet**: Ensure stable connection

### I see an error message

1. **Screenshot the error** for reference
2. **Try refreshing** the page
3. **Close and reopen** the app
4. **Clear cache** and try again
5. **Contact admin** with screenshot

### Page is loading slowly

1. **Check internet speed** - slow connection?
2. **Close other tabs/apps** - free up resources
3. **Restart browser**
4. **Try different browser**
5. **Check system resources** - enough RAM/CPU?

### "Double chrome" windows

This should be fixed! If you see windows with two title bars:
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Clear cache
3. If persists, report to admin (should not happen anymore)

### Features not working on mobile

Make sure you're using a supported browser (Safari on iOS, Chrome on Android). Some features are desktop-only (window snapping).

### Can't upload files

1. **Check file size** - under limit?
2. **Check file type** - supported format?
3. **Try smaller file** - test if size issue
4. **Different browser** - Chrome usually most reliable
5. **Check internet** - stable upload connection?

### Keyboard shortcuts not working

1. **Check correct modifier** - Cmd (Mac) vs Ctrl (Windows/Linux)
2. **Try in different app** - app-specific shortcuts
3. **Check for conflicts** - browser/OS override?
4. **Reload page** - reset state

### App looks broken / weird layout

1. **Zoom at 100%?** - Cmd+0 / Ctrl+0 to reset
2. **Browser up to date?** - Update to latest version
3. **Clear cache** - Old CSS might be cached
4. **Resize window** - Sometimes triggers re-layout
5. **Different browser** - Isolate issue

## Security & Privacy

### Is my data secure?

Yes! We use:
- **Encryption in transit** (HTTPS/TLS)
- **Encryption at rest** (database and file storage)
- **Secure authentication** (hashed passwords)
- **Regular security audits**
- **SOC 2 compliant infrastructure** (Abacus.AI)

### Who can see my information?

**Your Data:**
- **Personal**: Only you
- **Household**: You and admins
- **Messages**: Only you and recipients
- **Documents**: Based on folder permissions
- **Directory**: Name/unit visible to all, details to admins

**Admins Cannot See:**
- Your passwords
- Your private messages
- Your personal notes

### Can admins read my messages?

No! Private messages are truly private. Admins can see:
- Community announcements
- Support tickets
- System logs (who logged in when)

They cannot see private conversations.

### What if I suspect a security issue?

Contact your administrator immediately and:
1. **Change your password** right away
2. **Log out** of all devices
3. **Document** what you noticed
4. **Report** to support@abacus.ai if severe

### Is payment information secure?

Yes! Payments processed through Stripe (industry-leading security). We never store:
- Full credit card numbers
- CVV codes
- Unencrypted payment data

### Can I export my data?

Yes! Contact your admin to request data export in JSON format. Includes:
- Your profile information
- Your messages
- Your notes
- Your tasks
- Documents you uploaded

### GDPR / Privacy compliance?

Yes! The platform is GDPR-ready:
- Data export available
- Data deletion on request
- Clear privacy policies
- Consent management
- Audit logs
